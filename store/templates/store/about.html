Shirts

Material: Typically made from cotton, polyester, or a blend of both. Some T-shirts also use materials like linen, bamboo, or synthetic fibers for added comfort and durability.
Design: T-shirts come in a variety of designs, including plain, printed, graphic, and embellished styles. They may have short sleeves, long sleeves, or be sleeveless. Fit: Available in various fits such as slim fit, regular fit, and oversized.
Neck Style: Common neck styles include crew neck, V-neck, scoop neck, and polo neck.

Types of Watches:

Analog Watches:
Features: Traditional design with hour, minute, and sometimes second hands.
Use: Suitable for formal and casual wear.
Digital Watches:
Features: Display time in numerical format (HH).
Use: Ideal for sports and casual wear due to easy readability.
Smartwatches:
Features: Connect to smartphones, display notifications, track fitness metrics, GPS, heart rate monitoring.

Types of Shoes

Athletic Shoes: Designed for sports and physical activities. Examples include running shoes, basketball shoes, and soccer cleats.
Casual Shoes: Everyday footwear that is comfortable and versatile. Examples include sneakers, loafers, and boat shoes.
Formal Shoes: Designed for formal occasions and business settings. Examples include oxfords, brogues, and dress loafers.

Materials
Leather: Durable and flexible material often used in formal and high-quality shoes.
Canvas: Lightweight and breathable material used in casual shoes.
Synthetic: Man-made materials that can mimic leather, provide waterproofing, or add specific functionalities.
Rubber: Commonly used for soles due to its durability and grip.
